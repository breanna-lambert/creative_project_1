Menu

Y Delite

A favorite for vegetarians, the Y Delite is an all-veggie pizza featuring artichokes, broccoli, mushrooms, tomatoes, and olives. Our house-made tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese serve as the base.

BBQ

Our delicious BBQ pizza has a sweet honey barbecue sauce, mozzarella cheese, grilled chicken bites, cilantro, and red onion.

Chicken Alfredo

White alfredo sauce, a blend of three cheeses, chicken, broccoli, and a bit of red onion make the perfect flavor combination for this popular pizza.

Meat Lover's

If you came here looking for nothing but meat on your pizza, here's the one for you. Dive into this crowd-pleaser and taste the juicy ham, pepperoni, sausage, and bacon. Also uses our house-made tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese.
